# Manually Obtaining client_id and client_secret for oneindex

Many folks run into an issue where oneindex can’t obtain the `client_id` and `client_secret`.
This post explains how to manually get them.

## Get `client_id`

1. Open [Microsoft Azure](https://portal.azure.com/).
2. Log into the Microsoft account you’ll use for oneindex.
3. Click to manage **Azure Active Directory** and select **View**.
4. Go to **App registrations → New registration**.
5. Fill in **Name** and **Redirect URL**. For the **Redirect URL**, enter the URL that appears during oneindex installation.
6. For **Supported account types**, choose **Any organizational directory**, then click **Register**.
7. After registration, the **Application (client) ID** is your `client_id`.

## Get `client_secret`

1. Click **Certificates & secrets → New client secret**.
2. For **Expires**, choose **Never**, then click **Add**.
3. The **Value** is your `client_secret`.

## Some issues

- If installation fails, check whether the app is visible to **any organization**.
- After that, you can start using it smoothly.
